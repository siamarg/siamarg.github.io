ABOUT SIAMARG

Siamarg was founded by a group of women entrepreneurs with technology and mentoring support by Atal Incubation center - AIC ALEAP We-Hub (Supported by AIM, Niti Ayog, GOI). Our product is derived out of biobased raw materials which acts as an alternative to existing plastic products. The extensive use of petroleum-based plastic for over a century has resulted a major hazard towards the environment. The limited future availability of petroleum, the existing environment and waste management systems made us work on more sustainable alternatives to replace petroleum-derived plastic usage.

Our intention and contribution to the society is to minimize disposal of non-degradable petroleum-derived plastic waste by producing medical gears like PPE, Hospital Gowns, Aprons, Gloves, Masks, Garbage bags, Binliners, transportation bags, Medical shop pouches; packaging covers, holders, wraps, agricultural mulch films, horticultural pouches through biodegradable plastic as a raw material; thereby mitigating threat to our ecosystem."

Our vision is to become the leading brand in the bioplastic manufacturing industry offering sustainable alternatives to plastic consumers by leveraging innovative methods and sustainable solutions, thereby to establish a one stop biodegradable plastic product manufacturing company.
