Industrial

Siamarg Industrial grade or wrap is an industrial packaging substance composed of certified raw material from compostable and biodegradable corn starch made of renewable sources and a functional substitution for petrochemical raw materials, which require a large use of fossil fuels and conventional hydrocarbon resins.


The packaging industry depends on shrink wrapping because it provides various benefits such as durability, strength, recyclability and illustrative ability. This eco-friendly maize- based shrink films all have zero carbon footprints by reducing greenhouse gas emissions during production.


The retrenchment film provides a high shrinking ratio which needs less heat to compress without losing performances such as those of other films during production.

Garment Covers

Siamarg offers an appealing collection of sleek, fashionable, semi-transparent garment bags. The collection is made of corn based plastic and available in popular and modern styles. Motivated to reach full consumer acceptance, we sell BioPlastic Garment Bags the highest quality. The provided garment bags are known for their clear nature, lightweight and high strength and are used for packing garment bags. As a quality-oriented company, we ensure that we always retain the consistency of our garment bags.

  • Environmentally friendly

  • Savings electricity and costs
  • High mechanical strength and decreases film thickness
  • Fast decline and excellent stability
